Lemon, Garlic & Paprika Chicken

Kidney Friendly & Low Sodium

Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time 15 min
Servings 2 ppl

A delicious, kidney-friendly chicken dinner using fresh lemon and sweet paprika to boost flavor without adding salt or black pepper. Perfect for a renal diet.

Ingredients

    • For Serving

Directions

1

In a bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, oregano, and sweet paprika.

2

Coat the chicken breasts thoroughly in this mixture. Let it sit for 20 minutes if you have time to marinate, otherwise proceed immediately.

3

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

4

Cook the chicken for 6–7 minutes on each side until golden and cooked through.

Alternative: Bake at 200°C for 20–25 minutes.

5

Plate up with steamed white rice and green beans.

Kidney Health Note

This recipe controls Potassium and Phosphorus by using white rice and green beans instead of potatoes or tomato-based sauces. It is low sodium and avoids black pepper.

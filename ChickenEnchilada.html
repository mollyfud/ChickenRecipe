Chicken Enchiladas (Super Mild)

With homemade mild gravy and "Mock-amole" (Pea Guacamole).

Prep

20m

Cook

25m

Dietary Notes: Low Sodium, Low Potassium, Low Phosphorus. Uses corn tortillas and a homemade spice gravy instead of high-sodium canned sauces. Substitutes avocado with green peas to reduce potassium load.

Ingredients

The Filling

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup white rice, cooked
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 red capsicum (bell pepper), diced
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Mild Sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive or canola oil
  • 2 tbsp plain white flour
  • 1 tbsp sweet paprika (mild)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1.5 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste

Assembly

  • 8-10 Corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar
  • Fresh coriander & lime

Instructions

  1. 1

    Make the Filling

    In a pan, heat 1 tsp olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion and capsicum until soft (about 5 mins). In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken, cooked rice, and sautéed vegetables. Set aside.

  2. 2

    Make the Mild Sauce

    Heat 2 tbsp oil in a saucepan. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Stir in paprika, oregano, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and tomato paste.

    Slowly whisk in the low-sodium stock to remove lumps. Simmer 5–7 minutes until thickened.

  3. 3

    Assemble

    Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Wrap tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave for 30s to soften.

    Place a scoop of chicken/rice mixture in each tortilla, roll up, and place seam-side down in a baking dish.

  4. 4

    Bake

    Pour sauce over the enchiladas. Sprinkle lightly with cheese. Bake for 20 minutes. Garnish with lime and coriander.

Side: "Mock-amole" (Pea Guacamole)

A low-potassium alternative to avocado guacamole. Looks and feels just like the real thing!

Ingredients

  • • 2 cups Frozen peas
  • • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • • 1 tbsp Sour cream (optional)
  • • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • • 1/4 tsp Garlic powder
  • • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander

Instructions

Boil peas for 3-4 mins until very soft. Drain and rinse under cold water immediately. Blend all ingredients (except coriander) until smooth or chunky. Stir in chopped coriander. Serve chilled.
